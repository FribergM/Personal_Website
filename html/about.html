About Me

Hello! My name is Måns Friberg, a passionate Java developer currently studying and working toward a career in software development. I was born in 1995 in Stockholm, Sweden, as the youngest sibling in my family.

My journey into the world of technology began when I fell in love with video games at the age of 6, and since then, gaming has been a huge part of my life. This love for games sparked my interest in programming, leading me to pursue a deeper understanding of how things work behind the scenes. I am currently particularly drawn to back-end development.

Outside of tech, I'm all about traveling, hitting the golf course with my family, and enjoying some friendly competition. Whether it's exploring new places or competing in a game, I'm always up for a new adventure!
